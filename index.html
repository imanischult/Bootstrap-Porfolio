About Me

My name is Imani Schulterbrandt, and I am a Full Stack Web Developer.
Although I was born and raised in Los Angeles county, I currently reside in Atlanta, Ga. I have a Bachelor's Degree in Economics from the illustrious Spelman College, a historically black college (HBCU) in downtown Atlanta. I currently work for Delta Air Lines in the Cargo department, but intend on moving into a more technical role. I am skilled in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, node.js, and am working on mySQL, Express, and several other languages.
While at Delta Air Lines, I've grown a passion for traveling around the world (as the world becomes a backyard when working for an airline). In addition, I've acquired a hobby for trying different coffees in countries that I visit. I have two beautiful children and an amazing husband (all of who have their passports, of course).
